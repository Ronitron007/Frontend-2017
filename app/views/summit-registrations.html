Registrations


Welcome!

The Accessibility Summit will hold a Case Study Competition and a Social Enterprise Competition. Candidates can only register for one of the two. The last date for registration is November 12.

Registration for Social Enterprise Competition can be done in teams of up to 4 members. All members must register separately and must mention their team name in the registration form.

For Case Study Competition, students must register individually. Teams will be formed after selection.

You can register for Accessibility Summit here.

Based on the competition that the candidate registers for, a mail will be sent regarding further requirements for selection.

To know more about Accessibility Summit, please watch the teaser here.

Selection Procedure

The Selection Procedure post registration for the competitions will consist of two stages.

  • Social Enterprise Competition:

    1. The group members will be required to send an executive summary of their project idea and a 30 second video pitch.

    2. After clearing the first stage, a short interview will be conducted over Skype in the presence of all group members.
  • Case Study Competition:

    1. A problem statement will be sent to all registered candidates, and they will be required to send an original abstract.

    2. After clearing the first stage, a short interview will be conducted over Skype.

Registration Cost Structure

Once selected, the candidates will be required to pay a fee. The fee structure is as follows:

IITM students: ₹ 500
Other students: ₹ 1000
Nominal charges for accommodation have to be paid separately.
