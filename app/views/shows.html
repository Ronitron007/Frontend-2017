Formula DrOne

Shaastra in committed to fuel a sense of passion and interest in latest technological disruptions among the youth of this country. We are proud to present Formula DrOne, India’s first night Drone Racing Event in association with the Indian Drone Racing League.

FPV drone racing is a rapidly evolving ultra modern sport capturing imaginations of tech enthusiasts and geeks worldwide. Imagine flying around in a Tron like world with neon light streaks and markers all around. Your field of view, like that of a rebel pilot in Star Wars whizzing through the action. This is kind of what it feels like to be a pilot in a drone race.

This Shaastra get immersed in a futuristic atmosphere with drones whizzing past you at over 100Km/Hr in one of the most complicated race circuits ever built. Get ready to feel the 'a-drone-aline' rush this New Year as you watch India’s top pilots battle it out for ultimate glory ! Jaw dropping thrill is in store for the night of 2nd January along with a chance to experience the action first hand in a Virtual Drone Simulator.
