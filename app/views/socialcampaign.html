Pledge-A-Book (2014,2015):

Pledge-A-Book (PAB), was a unique social campaign to spread education, literacy, and multilingualism by facilitating the access of books to the underprivileged. Started in 2014, its main aim was to bridge the gap between those who have access to resources and those lacking them.

PAB was based on the simple yet powerful idea of mapping books that were no longer useful to the current owners, to underprivileged students.

Building on the success of the first edition, PAB was scaled up to a national level in 2015 by expanding on-ground as well as online presence.


Highlights:

  • We broadened our reach to 3 other cities (Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Pune) and collected over 15,000 books of which every book was mapped in our database.
  • 15 libraries were set up in and around Chennai.
  • An online portal was set up to link all the stakeholders.
  • Associations with 20+ NGOs, 10+ schools and more than 5 franchise associations like Crossword, Shell, Just Buy Cycles, etc. enabled a large number of people to donate books.
  • Limca Book Of Record: This record involved collection of about 12,000 customized Pledge-A-Book bookmarks from students of over 10 Kendriya Vidyalaya Schools within a span of 2 hours.
  • The campaign got support from celebrities like Sachin Tendulkar, Raghuram Rajan, Dalai Lama, Chetan Bhagat, R Ashwin, Kanan Gill, Kenny Sebastian, Abish Mathew, Akshar Patel, Stuart Binny, Rashmi Bansal, Saba Karim to name a few.

The libraries that we set up are fully functional today and are doing their bit to curb illiteracy, one library at a time.

We, at Shaastra are proud to be a socially responsible fest and we hope to take this legacy forward in 2017 through SYNK.
