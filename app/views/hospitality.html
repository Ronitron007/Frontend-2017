FAQ

1) What about accommodation?

Accommodation to participants will be provided to outstation participants if they have applied for accommodation on the Shaastra website and their accommodation has been approved by us. The rooms will be allotted on a sharing basis due to space constraints. We have common room, and single rooms for accommodation. We will be providing essential commodities like Mattresses and pillows will be provided.

Accommodation will be provided between 31st of December 2016, 12:00 Noon to 3rd of January 2017, 08:00 PM only. No further adjustments are possible with regard to the timeframe.

The participants who have not been confirmed for accommodation are requested to arrange for their stay outside the Institute in Hotels or lodges.


2) Will all the team members be given accommodation at the same place?

We will try but due to space restrictions we cannot guarantee for the same.


3) What about the charges for accommodation?

A nominal payment of around ₹350 per participant per night will have to be made by participants for accommodation while registering at control room. Also a caution deposit of ₹1000 per participant has to be made by the participant which will be returned to the participant on the return of all commodities provided to him such as mattresses and pillows.


4) What is the Cancellation Policy with regard to accommodation at Shaastra 2017?

Information regarding this will be updated on the website soon.


5) Can we adjust the remaining team members of our team in the space provided to us?

No, the members of your team who have not been provided accommodation cannot stay in your allotted rooms. They are requested to stay at nearby hotels or lodges outside the campus.


6) One of our team members who had been provided accommodation is unable to come so can we replace him with our other team member?

First, kindly get approval for replacement of your old team member from the coordinator of the event in which your team member was supposed to participate. After consulting with the event coordinator we will inform you whether we will be able to accommodate your new team member. The new team member coming should have all the documents that the other member was supposed to have.


7) I still have unanswered queries, what should I do?

You can contact us by mailing to studentrelations@shaastra.org. Any queries you might have can be directed to our team by phone. We look forward to having you at Shaastra 2017 and hope that you have a marvelous time!
