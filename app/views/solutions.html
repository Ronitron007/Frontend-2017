ABOUT

Shaastra Solutions is an initiative which aims at bridging the gap between universities and industries.The jump from college to industry is usually a huge one for the college student. This is mainly due to lack of experience to working environment in the industrial sector. Shaastra Solutions outsources problems/assignments faced by companies to undergraduate students all across India. Out of the applicants, a rigorous selection process will choose the select few participants. These high quality participants will be grouped into teams and assigned various targets/tasks by companies to complete, in a span of 4-6 weeks. Projects are taken from a variety of fields to provide ample opportunity for people with varying talents to participate in whatever fields they choose. In addition to all this Shaastra Solutions will act as an effective communication bridge between company and team, ensuring the fulfilment of both their interests
