ABOUT

Shaastra Solutions is an initiative which aims at bridging the gap between universities and industries.The jump from college to industry is usually a huge one for the college student. This is mainly due to lack of experience to working environment in the industrial sector. Shaastra Solutions outsources problems/assignments faced by companies to undergraduate students all across India. Out of the applicants, a rigorous selection process will choose the select few participants. These high quality participants will be grouped into teams and assigned various targets/tasks by companies to complete, in a span of 4-6 weeks. Projects are taken from a variety of fields to provide ample opportunity for people with varying talents to participate in whatever fields they choose. In addition to all this Shaastra Solutions will act as an effective communication bridge between company and team, ensuring the fulfilment of both their interests

Shaastra Solutions in collaboration with Team Nimbus is proud to present to you india’s first crowd sourced​ event.

The purpose of this event is to crowdsource solutions for different modules of an Unmanned Aerial Vehicle planned to compete in an international aero-design competition.

  • Since the landing gear cannot be retracted, the landing gear must cause minimal drag. Which landing gear configuration would be most suitable?
  • Keeping in mind the weight restrictions of the plane, which material could be used for the landing gear?
  • Conceptualize a wing structure that can withstand a force of 40 Newtons on each side. The wing must incorporate sweep and a high taper ratio of 0.45. It is an EPPLER 423 airfoil.
  • Design an engine module to start the IC engine in mid-flight.
  • Assuming that the payload must be located at either the front or back of the plane. What would be the most suitable location to place the payload?
  • Plane Dimensions
  • Right wing for simulation
