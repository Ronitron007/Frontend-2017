Events


Case Study Competition: The Case Study Competition aims to bring to focus the obstacles with regard to Accessibility in India. Participants will get the opportunity to work on problem statements provided by professional organizations tailored from real-life challenges. They will be required to work in groups with students from different backgrounds. The objective is to analyze the problem, discuss existent models, and present a workable solution.

Social Enterprise Competition: This event is targeted towards students interested in entrepreneurship in the social sector.It will be a chance for innovative individuals to devise a profitable and practical business model intended to improve accessibility in terms of technology, service delivery, legal aid etc., and find methods to implement it.

Hackathon for the Visually Impaired: This is a platform for visually impaired students to demonstrate their proficiency in computer science and programming. It aims to bring to focus the immense potential for employability and independence that people with disabilities have but is often ignored. To bring out the best in the participants,International Accessibility Summit has collaborated with Vision-Aid to teach visually impaired students the basics of programming. For this, students from IIT Madras have volunteered to act as teaching assistants.

Central Panel Discussion: The Summit will host a panel comprised of experts working in different ways to make the country more accessible. The panelists are from varied fields -- policy making, assistive technology, corporate social responsibility, and the social sector. The discussion will bring to light the world standards for inclusivity and how India can match up to them.

Lectures and Workshops: Participants will be exposed to different aspects of the theme of Accessibility through lectures covering topics of policy development and assistive technology. The participants will also take part in a Sensitization workshop to break the social stigma against disability and provide an in-depth understanding of the topic of inclusion and accessibility.They will also take part in a Web Accessibility workshop to understand accessibility of Information technology.
