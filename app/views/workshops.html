Registrations:

  1. People who are interested in attending workshops are requested to register for the corresponding workshops in the registration page.
  2. Robotics Workshops - Hand controlled Bot, Paired robotics, Object Tracking Bot and SPAD accepts registrations only in teams of 5 people at maximum.
  3. For all the other workshops, registrations should be done individually or in teams of maximum 3 people.

After Registration:

  1. Following registrations, a questionnaire for each workshop is to be filled.
  2. In case of team registrations, ALL the team members are required to fill the questionnaire and upload their submissions in one doc.
  3. Submissions are to be uploaded in the registration page by clicking on ‘Upload Questionnaire’ for workshops.
  4. Registrants who are selected for the workshop will get confirmation mails.
  5. Confirmed registrants are requested to pay for the workshop stamp (INR 300) + kit charge(if it is a kit workshop) to confirm their participation.

Note:

  • Workshop stamp is necessary for all workshops.
  • In case of getting confirmed for multiple workshops, paying once for workshop stamp is enough.
  • Refunds will not be entertained in case of wrong payments.
