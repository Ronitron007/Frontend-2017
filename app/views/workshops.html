Registrations:

  • People who are interested in attending workshops are requested to register for the corresponding workshops in the registration page.
  • Robotics Workshops - Hand controlled Bot, Paired robotics, Object Tracking Bot and SPAD accepts registrations only in teams of 5 people at maximum.
  • For all the other workshops, registrations should be done individually or in teams of maximum 3 people.

After Registration :

  • Following registrations, a questionnaire for each workshop is to be filled.
  • In case of team registrations, ALL the team members are required to fill the questionnaire and upload their submissions in one doc.
  • Submissions are to be uploaded in the registration page by clicking on ‘Upload Questionnaire’ for workshops.
  • Registrants who are selected for the workshop will get confirmation mails.
  • Confirmed registrants are requested to pay for the workshop passport + kit charge(if it is a kit workshop) to confirm their participation.

Note:

  • Workshop passport is necessary for all workshops.
  • In case of getting confirmed for multiple workshops, paying once for workshop passport is enough.
  • Refunds will not be entertained in case of wrong payments.
