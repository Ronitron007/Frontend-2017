Payment Information


Workshops payment:

Base charge (INR 300) + Workshop module charges as applicable for the workshop your are registered and selected for.

Every person attending any of the workshop needs to pay a base charge of INR 300.

Workshops for which additional module charges are applicable:

All the industrial workshops

All the robotics workshops

The applicable module amount for each of these workshops is mentioned in the tab for that particular workshop on the website.

Step wise Procedure to register and payment:

  1. Go to SBI icollect here.
  2. Select "Tamil Nadu" for State of Corporate/Institution option and "Educational Institutions" for Type of Corporate/Institution option.
  3. Select "IITM STUDENTS ACTIVITIES TRUST-SHAASTRA" for Educational Institutions Name option.
  4. Select "Workshop Stamp - 2017" for Select Payment Category option.
  5. Fill in your details and proceed for payment.
  6. Repeat the procedure to pay for the workshop you are attending ( if charges applicable).
  7. You need to repeat the procedure multiple times, in case you are attending multiple workshops.

Accomodation :

Payments for accommodation will be done on-spot during Shaastra.

Every person having approval for accommodations need to pay at the spot for accommodations. These payments are completely independent of payments for workshops.

FAQs:

Question: What does the approval for accommodation mean exactly?

Answer: Shaastra Student Relations Team will send a confirmation mail to all the people who have applied for accommodation(Ticked the accommodation checkbox while registration) and have responded positively. This mail will reach you on or before 20/12/2016.
